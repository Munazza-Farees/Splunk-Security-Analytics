Queries

1. Top HELO/EHLO domains

index=smtp_logs 
| stats count by helo_ehlo
| sort - count

2. SMTP reply code distribution

index=smtp_logs smtp_code=*
| stats count by smtp_code
| sort - count

3. Success vs Failure pie 

index=smtp_logs smtp_code=*
| eval status=case(match(smtp_code,"^2"),"Success",
                   match(smtp_code,"^(4|5)"),"Failure",
                   true(),"Other")
| stats count by status
